MIN -> Minutes - The number of minutes a player or team has played.
PTS -> Points - The number of points a player or team has scored. A point is scored when a player make a basket.
FGM -> Field Goals Made - The number of field goals that a player or team has made. This includes both 2 pointers and 3 pointers.
FGA -> Field Goals Attempted - The number of filed goals that a player or team has attempted. This includes both 2 pointers and 3 pointers.
FG% -> Field Goal Percentage - The percentage of field goals that a player makes. The formula to determine field goal percentage is: Field Goals Made/Field Goals Attempted.
3PM -> 3 Point Field Goals Made - The number of 3 point field goals that a player or team has made.
3PA -> 3 Point Field Goals Attempted - The number of 3 point field field goals that a player or team has attempted.
3P% -> 3 Point Field Goal Percentage - The percentage of 3 point field goals that a player or team has made. The formula to determine 3 point field goal percentage is: 3 Point Field Goals Made/3 Point Field Goals Attempted.
FTM -> Free Throws Made - The number of free throws that a player or team has successfully made.
FTA -> Free Throws Attempted - The number of free throws that a player or team has taken.
FT% -> Free Throw Percentage - The percentage of free throws that a player or team has made. The formula to determine free throw percentage is: Free Throws Made/Free Throws Attempted.
OREB -> Offensive Rebounds - The number of rebounds a player or team has collected while they were on offense.
DREB -> Defensive Rebounds - The number of rebounds a player or team has collected while they were on defense.
REB -> Rebounds - A rebound occurs when a player recovers the ball after a missed shot. This statistic is the number of total rebounds a player or team has collected on either offense or defense.
AST -> Assists - An assist occurs when a player completes a pass to a teammate that directly leads to a made field goal.
STL -> Steals - A steal occurs when a defensive player takes the ball from a player on offese, causing a turnover.
BLK -> Blocks - A block occurs when an offensive player attempts a shot, and the defense player tips the ball, blocking their chance to score.
TOV -> Turnovers - A turnover occurs when the team on offense loses the ball to the defense.
PF -> Personal Fouls - The total number of fouls that a player or team has committed.
+/- -> The point differential of the score for a player while on the court. For a team, it is how much they are winning or losing by.
